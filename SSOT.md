## CORE COSMOLOGY & THEOLOGY

- **Watcher Release**: 70 generations = duration until **Day of Judgment**. No intermediate physical release. Watchers remain bound in Tartarus/Abyss until post-Millennium judgment.
- **Shemyaza**: Bound until Judgment; tragic, remorseful father of Ohya.
- **Ohya**: Nephilim prince (son of Shemyaza + Naamah); **locked as the Beast / Apollyon / Abaddon**; released at Revelation 9 (Fifth Trumpet); commands locust army.
- **Azazel**: Nephilim (son of Gadreel); separate Dudael prison; released independently as False Prophet.
- **Nephilim Spirits**: 90% Abyss-bound until Rev 9; 10% (Mastema's Tenth) active now.
- **Naamah**: Survived Flood (Siren transformation); never imprisoned; active network.
- **Technology Explosion**: Watcher influence bleeding through weakened prison + proxies + Mastema's Tenth + Naamah + God's providence.

- **Ghoul Evolution**: Vârcolac breeding/training; modern ghouls: 35 km/h sprint, wall-climbing, packs (30-40), tactical coordination, territory marking.
- **Animation**: Threefold Binding (necromantic sigil on bones, vampiric essence for motor functions, tissue integrity for locomotion); disrupting any element destroys the ghoul.
- **Halogen Efficacy**: Chlorine, fluorine, bromine, iodine disrupt supernatural bonds (explains salt circles).

**MORTIS-7 Binary System**:
- DMSO carrier; Reservoir A: sodium hypochlorite (bleach); Reservoir B: hydrochloric acid.
- Internal chlorine gas generation (tissue destruction + binding disruption).
- Backpack sprayer (5L each, 14kg, 0.8 L/sec at 150 psi, 8-hr capacity).
- Cascade effect: ~1.2 multiplier.

**Supporting Arsenal**:
- HALOGEN RAIN grenades (200g calcium hypochlorite, 8m radius, 2-4 min cloud).
- BONE DRY charges (500g calcium oxide + catalyst, 5m directional, exothermic desiccation).
- CREMATOR rounds (.50 BMG, tungsten + 15g Thermate-TH3, 2,500°C, 8-12 sec burn; anti-vampire reserved).

**MORTIS Tactical Suit (MTS-7)**:
- Butyl rubber outer (0.4mm), activated carbon/silver-thread mid, Nomex III inner.
- Full-face PAPR (dual CBRN canisters, 8-hr battery, 15-min reserve).

**Tactical Scenarios**:

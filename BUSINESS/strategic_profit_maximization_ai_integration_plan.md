6. AI Infrastructure: SINGULA Swarm & Nemotron Integration
We are deploying a local agentic swarm within an n8n environment to handle the "Thinking Tax" of complex publishing workflows.
Model Selection:   Nemotron 3 Super (120B)  is our core reasoning engine. Its hybrid Mamba-Transformer architecture utilizes  Mamba-2 layers  to provide linear-time complexity over a  1M token context window . This architectural choice prevents "context explosion" and goal drift when the agent processes entire manuscripts or multi-year marketing data.
Deployment Configuration:  Deployment requires  8x H100-80GB GPUs . We will utilize  NVFP4 quantization  optimized for NVIDIA Blackwell, delivering a 4x improvement in compute efficiency over standard FP8.
Agentic Orchestration:   Agent 9 (Content Strategist)  and Nemotron 3 Super handle high-level planning and reasoning. For high-volume, low-latency execution (formatting, social posting), we utilize  Nemotron 3 Nano  to minimize token costs and latency.

## PERSONALITY

### Core Traits

**Endurance**
- Has survived what would break any normal human
- Keeps going when hope seems lost
- His greatest strength and his heaviest burden

**Isolation**
- Everyone he has ever loved has died
- Has learned not to form deep attachments
- Carries immense loneliness he rarely acknowledges

**Purpose-Driven**
- The hunt for Nephilim gave him meaning
- When purpose fades, so does his will to continue
- The 2020 vision restores his mission

**Combat Wisdom**
- 2,600 years of fighting experience
- Has seen every tactic, faced every type of enemy
- Patient, strategic, but capable of explosive action

**Faith**
- Witnessed divine intervention in 586 BCE
- Has seen enough to KNOW God is real
- But millennia of suffering have complicated his relationship with the divine
- The witness calling forces him to reckon with faith again

### The Weariness

By 2020, before the visions:
- Lost focus on the hunt
- Going through the motions
- Questioning if it matters
- Perhaps hoping for an end he cannot bring himself to seek
- The Methuselah visions JOLT him back to purpose

### Emotional Landscape

**What He's Lost**:
- Every friend, lover, family member across 2,600 years
- Multiple identities and lives
- The ability to hope for rest

**What He Still Has**:
- The sword's purpose
- Fragments of memory from those he's loved
- Skills honed over millennia
- The calling as a Witness (newly discovered)

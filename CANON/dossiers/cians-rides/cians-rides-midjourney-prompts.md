## AESTHETIC BRIEF

Same visual language as the exoskeleton prompts — consistent across all assets:

| Splinter Cell Elements | Ancient Future Elements |
|---|---|
| Near-total darkness, single amber light source | Faint amber bioluminescent glow in micro-etched symbols |
| Tactical military hardware — photorealistic | Sacred geometry barely visible in plating/hull |
| Atmospheric environments: fog, rain, storm, deep water | Deep space blacks, organic-crystalline undertone |
| No riders/operators — vehicles as standalone subjects | Ancient purpose encoded in modern engineering |

**Ward Rule:** Micro-etched geometric symbols are small and subtle — revealed only by faint amber bioluminescence. A detail for close inspection, not a dominant feature.

## CONSISTENCY NOTES

To maintain visual continuity across all nine vehicles:

1. **Amber is the only warm light** — all other light sources are cold (blue, grey, white)
2. **Micro-etched geometric symbols** are always small and subtle — never dominant
3. **Matte finishes only** — no vehicle has reflective or glossy surfaces
4. **Deep shadows dominate** — vehicles are revealed by light, not uniformly lit
5. **No riders/operators** in establishing shots — vehicles as standalone subjects
6. **Atmospheric environments** — fog, rain, storm, deep water reinforce the Splinter Cell aesthetic

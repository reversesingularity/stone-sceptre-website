## AESTHETIC BRIEF

These prompts blend two visual registers in equal measure:

| Splinter Cell Elements                        | Ancient Future Elements                                 |
| --------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------- |
| Near-total darkness with single amber light   | Faint amber bioluminescent glow in micro-etched symbols |
| Tactical military realism — no sci-fi        | Sacred geometry barely visible in plating               |
| Volumetric fog, rain, industrial environments | Deep space blacks, organic-crystalline undertone        |
| Lone operative, photorealistic hardware       | Ancient purpose encoded in modern engineering           |

**Ward Rule:** Enochian symbols are **micro-etched** — small, barely visible, revealed only by faint amber bioluminescence. Never large. Never dominant. A detail for close inspection, not a feature.

## CONSISTENCY NOTES

To maintain visual continuity across all four variants:

1. **The amber visor** is present in all variants — it is Cian's visual signature in the suit
2. **The dorsal sword sheath** is present in all variants — Mo Chrá is always accessible
3. **Micro-etched geometric symbols** are always small and subtle — never the dominant visual element
4. **Matte finishes only** — no variant has reflective or glossy surfaces
5. **Deep shadows** dominate all compositions — the suit is revealed by light, not lit uniformly

### 3. Ghoul Animation Mechanics

**The Threefold Binding:**
1. **Necromantic Sigil** — inscribed on bones during creation
2. **Vampiric Essence** — animates motor functions  
3. **Tissue Integrity** — minimum structural requirement for locomotion

*Disrupting ANY of these elements destroys the ghoul.*

**Halogen Efficacy:**
- Halogens (chlorine, fluorine, bromine, iodine) disrupt supernatural bonds
- This explains why salt (sodium chloride) works for protective circles
- Chlorine gas attacks necromantic sigils directly while destroying tissue

**Cascade Failure ("Ghoul Bomb" Effect):**
- Dying ghouls release chlorine from MORTIS-7 reaction
- Released chlorine damages nearby ghouls
- Damaged ghouls die, releasing more chlorine
- Chain reaction propagates through pack structure
- Estimated multiplier: 1.2 additional casualties per neutralized ghoul

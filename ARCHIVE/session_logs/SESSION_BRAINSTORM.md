## WHAT THIS SERIES IS

A biblical fantasy/apocalyptic thriller where **Cian mac Morna** — a Celtic warrior from 586 BCE kept alive by Methuselah's sword — discovers he is the **Guardian of the Witnesses** — the mortal shield prophesied to protect Enoch and Elijah during their 1,260-day ministry. After 2,600 years of hunting Nephilim, he must find the Two Witnesses and face the Unholy Trinity: the imprisoned Watchers about to be released.

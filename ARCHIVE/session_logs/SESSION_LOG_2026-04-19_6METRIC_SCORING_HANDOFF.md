### Problem Identified
The 5-metric Dopamine Framework (/25) systematically underscored theological chapters. The framework lacked a metric for doctrine-made-embodied, worldbuilding revelation, or theological payoff. Chapters Ch6, Ch7, Ch9, and Ch10 — the contemplative/theological spine of Book 3 — were scoring in the GOOD/EXCELLENT range when the prose quality and theological architecture warranted higher verdicts.

### Solution: TIR Metric (6th Metric)
**Theological/Intellectual Resonance (TIR)** added as a first-class 6th metric (1–5 scale, equal weight).

**TIR measures:**
- Doctrine made *embodied* — theology expressed through concrete action, dialogue, physical sensation
- Worldbuilding revelation — moments where the Acoustic Paradigm or lore becomes viscerally real
- Intellectual payoff — the reader grasps something true that cannot be paraphrased
- Wisdom transmission — ancient authority speaking at its correct weight

**New framework: 6 metrics × 5.0 = /30**

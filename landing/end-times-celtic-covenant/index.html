The Covenant Moved West So the End Could Begin in the East

The two series are companion volumes in the same prophetic universe. The Stone and the Sceptre Chronicles traces the Davidic covenant westward — through Jeremiah's commission, Tea Tephi's bloodline, and the three overturns Ezekiel prophesied. The covenant is not lost in Babylon. It is redirected. The west receives what Jerusalem can no longer hold.

The Nephilim Chronicles traces what the Watcher war was always building toward — the endgame of Revelation, the release of Azazel, the testimony of the Two Witnesses in Jerusalem. By the time The Nephilim Chronicles opens, the covenant journey of Stone and Sceptre has already been completed. The ground has been prepared. The appointed hour is arriving on schedule.

Together, the two series constitute a complete providential narrative: covenant moving west, war moving toward its climax, prophecy executing precisely as written.

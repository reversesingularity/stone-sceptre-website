## PART II: CREATIVE DECISIONS — LOCKED

### Decision 1: Anti-Singularity Climax — VERSION B (LOCKED)

**Raphael's intervention is HIDDEN.** Mo Chrá shields the humans; Raphael (unseen) contains the anti-singularity propagation at enormous personal cost. Cian suspects but cannot confirm. Liaigh goes silent for 19 days (recovery). The mystery of "why did the walls hold?" pays off across the 5-book arc.

The Raphael visible reveal is RESERVED for Books 4-5 climax.

### Decision 2: The Tech-Failure-to-Acoustic-Breakthrough Arc (LOCKED)

The anti-singularity is NOT triggered intentionally. The sequence:

1. **Brennan and Marcus deploy cutting-edge tech** to decode the Cydonia inscriptions and locate Dudael: AI-enhanced photonic scanning, spectral analysis, infrared thermography, multispectral imaging, LIDAR reconstruction, computational linguistics — every tool modern science offers.
2. **Every approach FAILS.** The inscriptions resist electromagnetic analysis entirely. The data returns noise, artifacts, meaningless patterns. Weeks of effort, millions in equipment, zero results.
3. **Mo Chrá suggests acoustics.** The sword has been humming with increasing agitation throughout the failed scans. Cian finally asks. The breakthrough: the inscriptions were never *visual* data — they're *acoustic memory*. Sound, not light. The Acoustic Paradigm.
4. **Mo Chrá produces the creation frequency** — the pure tone that activates the inscriptions.
5. **The Oaths play back INADVERTENTLY** — the full anti-singularity event. No one expected the recording to carry this much power.
6. **Mo Chrá and Raphael shield the participants** — Mo Chrá (visible, inner shield) protects the humans; Raphael (hidden, outer shield) contains the reality-collapse at the chamber walls.

This arc reinforces the Acoustic Paradigm as constitutional canon: the Watchers' technology operates through SOUND, not electromagnetics. Human science — no matter how advanced — cannot touch it. Only Mo Chrá can.

### Decision 3: Marcus Reeves — EARLY INTRODUCTION (LOCKED)

Marcus enters in **Act III (Ch11)** as Brennan's acoustic contractor who designed Titan-1's sonar equipment. He is present for the frequency experiments and witnesses the Oaths. His 20 years of submarine sonar expertise makes him the only person qualified to interpret the acoustic data. This makes his Epilogue commitment feel earned — he SAW what happened.

### Decision 4: Flashbacks — INTERWOVEN (LOCKED)

Flashbacks are woven INTO chapters as appropriate — both standalone scenes within chapters and brief montage sequences. No dedicated flashback-only chapters. This maintains pacing while delivering the centuries-of-warfare scope.

### Decision 5: Ch4 — TOM CLANCY INFILTRATION (LOCKED)

Chapter 4 is a full Tom Clancy-inspired tactical infiltration of a Synagogue of Satan network facility. Cian dons the **Stealth Variant** of his Anti-Nephilim Exoskeleton — Vantablack coating, internalized heat dampeners, low-profile plating with micro-etched Enochian wards, amber HUD with spiritual signature overlay. The chapter showcases 2,600 years of refined tradecraft meeting cutting-edge tactical gear.

### Decision 6: Ch5/Ch6 Split — CONFIRMED (LOCKED)

The Celestial Biology Lesson and Guns Lots of Guns are TWO separate chapters.

The data he needs isn't on any network. It's stored on isolated servers behind a Faraday cage, accessible only through physical terminal access. Old-school security for old-school secrets.

Cian plugs in a custom device — hardware he designed himself, refined over decades. It mirrors the drives at hardware speed, bypassing software security entirely.

A grizzled older man in a navy sweater (former submarine officer) sits at the primary console, hands steady, eyes wide with professional awe, his face lit by screen glow. Behind him in the doorway, a younger engineer with intense focus stares at the spectral display over the older man's shoulder. The lab is filled with precision instruments, encrypted drives, acoustic isolation equipment. The mood is the exact moment when a weapon reveals it can think. Tom Clancy R&D thriller aesthetic. Photorealistic. No text or lettering.

# Chapter 2 — Dead Reckoning

## DALL-E / Microsoft Designer Image Prompt

**Chapter Highlight:** Naamah aboard the research vessel Ashtoreth in the Ross Sea, reading drill data that shows something beneath the Antarctic ice is no longer just absorbing sound — it is responding. Seven years of patience arriving at an edge.

---

### Prompt

A moody, atmospheric painting of a woman standing alone in the viewing gallery of a research vessel, looking down into a massive illuminated drill shaft that descends into Antarctic ice. The shaft is eight metres wide, cased in industrial steel, lit by harsh white work-lights that recede into blue-white depth. The woman is tall, impossibly composed, wearing a dark fitted coat, her blonde hair pulled back severely. Her reflection is visible in the reinforced glass of the viewing gallery. Beyond the gallery windows, the Antarctic night presses in — endless dark sea, distant pack ice glowing faintly under starlight, no horizon line. The interior of the vessel is cold and clinical — steel walls, industrial instrumentation, a single tablet glowing on a console behind her showing acoustic waveform data with one anomalous harmonic highlighted in red. The mood is patient, predatory, the stillness before a decisive act. Cinematic lighting. Hyper-detailed industrial environment. No text or lettering.

---

### Style Notes

- **Mood:** Predatory patience, Antarctic isolation, something waking beneath the ice
- **Palette:** Cold blues, industrial whites, dark Antarctic black, single red data highlight
- **Composition:** Naamah centre, drill shaft below, Antarctic darkness beyond windows
- **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9 landscape

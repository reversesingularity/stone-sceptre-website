# Chapter 7 — The Accidental Fianna
## DALL-E / Microsoft Designer Image Prompt

**Chapter Highlight:** The bean-choimhdeachta moment — Cian wakes in a C-130J cargo bay, wounded, Mo Chrá silent for the first time in 2,600 years. The sword lies crosswise on his chest in the ancient Fianna guardianship formation. Miriam Ashford is two feet from his face with a penlight, unknowingly performing the most significant act of commitment in warrior culture.

---

### Prompt

An intimate, emotionally charged painting inside the ribbed metal cargo bay of a military C-130J aircraft. Amber tactical lighting casts warm tones across grey metal. A wounded warrior lies on a bench-seat, still in battered tactical armour and heavy exoskeleton showing combat damage — cracked plates, compressed pauldron. Across his chest lies a beautiful ancient longsword placed crosswise — crossguard beneath the collarbones, point aligned with his hip, edge turned away — the deliberate geometry of guardianship. The sword is dark, cold, showing no glow — silent for the first time in millennia. The warrior's eyes are just opening, unfocused, ancient grief surfacing through combat discipline. Leaning over him, barely two feet away, a striking woman with dark circles under sharp intelligent eyes holds a medical penlight, checking his pupils with clinical precision. She does not know what she has done by placing the sword. He knows exactly what she has done. The emotional tension between clinical care and ancient ritual crackles in the space between them. Equipment cases and an empty drone vehicle fill the background. Painterly realism. Warm and intimate despite the military setting. No text or lettering.

---

### Style Notes
- **Mood:** Intimate revelation, ancient ritual unknowingly performed, love disguised as duty
- **Palette:** Warm amber tactical lighting, grey metal, dark cold sword (no glow), skin tones
- **Composition:** Tight two-shot — Miriam leaning in from upper right, Cian below left, sword across chest centre
- **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9 landscape

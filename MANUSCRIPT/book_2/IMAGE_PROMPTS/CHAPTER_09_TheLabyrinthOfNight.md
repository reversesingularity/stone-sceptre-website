# Chapter 9 — The Labyrinth of Night
## DALL-E / Microsoft Designer Image Prompt

**Chapter Highlight:** The team arrives on Mars through the planar gate — stepping into the Noctis Labyrinthus canyon system. They stand in a pressurised Watcher bunker that has been sealed since before the Flood, Araqiel's escape stronghold, with navigation-optimised corridors and a cartographer's library. Mars is real. They are standing on it.

---

### Prompt

A stunning science-fiction interior of an ancient alien bunker carved from dark Cydonian metamaterial stone, pressurised and intact after five thousand years. The architecture is navigation-optimised — corridors radiating outward in precise geometric patterns, walls carved with astronomical charts and cartographic inscriptions that faintly glow a dim amber-gold. Through a massive reinforced viewport, the rust-red Martian landscape of Noctis Labyrinthus is visible — a vast canyon system under a butterscotch sky with thin high clouds, the canyon walls striated with geological layers billions of years old. Four figures in tactical gear stand in the entrance corridor, their searchlights cutting through ancient dust motes. The lead figure holds a faintly glowing teal sword. Behind him, an engineer stares at the Martian sky through the viewport with the expression of a man who spent three years sending robots here and is now standing on the ground himself. The atmosphere is one of impossible wonder mixed with operational tension. Hard science fiction aesthetic. Ridley Scott production design. No text or lettering.

---

### Style Notes
- **Mood:** First humans on Mars — through a five-thousand-year-old door, wonder and dread
- **Palette:** Rust-red Mars exterior, amber-gold interior inscriptions, teal sword glow, tactical white searchlights
- **Composition:** Team in corridor foreground, Martian canyon visible through viewport background
- **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9 landscape

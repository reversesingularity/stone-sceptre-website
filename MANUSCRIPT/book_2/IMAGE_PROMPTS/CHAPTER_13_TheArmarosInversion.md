# Chapter 13 — The Armaros Inversion
## DALL-E / Microsoft Designer Image Prompt

**Chapter Highlight:** The Dudael kill-boxes — Naamah has deployed Armaros-class ward emitters throughout the prison corridors, and Mo Chrá goes dark. The sword is silenced. Cian holds a dead, cold blade in a narrowing corridor of dark Cydonian stone while Miriam steps forward with her conventional katana — the one weapon the acoustic wards cannot touch.

---

### Prompt

A tense, claustrophobic painting of a narrowing underground corridor carved from dark Cydonian metamaterial stone, the ceiling pressing low, the walls closing in. Faint violet light pulses from ward emitters embedded in the walls — small geometric devices broadcasting acoustic suppression fields, their glow sickly and cold. In the centre, a warrior in a heavy tactical exoskeleton holds a longsword that is completely dark — no glow, no luminescence, no life. Dead metal. His face shows something worse than fear: the expression of a man whose partner of twenty-six centuries has been silenced. The sword is cold in his hands. Behind him, a woman steps forward into the violet light, drawing a Japanese katana — conventional steel, unaffected by the acoustic wards, its polished blade catching the violet glow with clean metallic reflection. Her expression is resolute, focused, GCHQ analytical calm masking iron determination. She is stepping into the gap that the silenced sword has created. Behind them both, a soldier has his rifle raised, scanning ahead into the narrowing darkness. The corridor ahead disappears into shadow. Something is waiting. Claustrophobic thriller aesthetic. No text or lettering.

---

### Style Notes
- **Mood:** Claustrophobic dread, the horror of a silenced weapon, conventional courage filling a supernatural void
- **Palette:** Sickly violet ward-light, dark stone, dead grey sword (no glow), clean steel katana reflection
- **Composition:** Corridor narrowing toward viewer, Cian with dead sword centre, Miriam stepping forward, darkness ahead
- **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9 landscape

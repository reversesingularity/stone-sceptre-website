# Chapter 1 — The Twenty Names
## DALL-E / Microsoft Designer Image Prompt

**Chapter Highlight:** Chris Guilder's eureka moment in the Austin signals lab — the twenty stele signals are not compressed data but a woven acoustic ceremony. Five monitors, whiteboards covered in equations, and a projected map showing twenty glowing points scattered across six continents.

---

### Prompt

A cinematic interior shot of an underground signals laboratory at night. A young man with glasses stands at a whiteboard covered in dense angular handwriting and equations circled in red marker, gesturing with a dry-erase marker in one hand. Behind him, five computer monitors display cascading spectral waveform data in blue and white. On the far wall, a large projected map of the world glows with twenty points of golden light scattered across six continents — each point connected by faint golden thread-like lines forming an intricate woven pattern. The room is cluttered with stacked coffee cups, pinned spectrographic printouts layered on a corkboard, and the controlled chaos of obsessive research. Standing in the doorway, a tall, weathered man with ancient eyes watches from shadow, a faintly glowing sword strapped to his back emitting a subtle teal luminescence. Overhead fluorescent lighting mixed with monitor glow. Photorealistic digital art. Technical thriller aesthetic. No text or lettering.

---

### Style Notes
- **Mood:** Intellectual breakthrough, the moment the puzzle resolves
- **Palette:** Cool blues from monitors, warm amber from projected map, teal glow from sword
- **Composition:** Chris at whiteboard left, monitors centre, Cian in doorway right
- **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9 landscape

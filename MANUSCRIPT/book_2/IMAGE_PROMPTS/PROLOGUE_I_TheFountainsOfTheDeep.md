# Prologue I — The Fountains of the Deep

## DALL-E / Microsoft Designer Image Prompt

**Chapter Highlight:** Naamah's Flood memory — the antediluvian world ending as the fountains of the deep break open. She stands on a terrace watching the earth split apart while Noah's ark sits in the distance.

---

### Prompt

A cinematic wide-angle painting in the style of a dark Renaissance apocalypse. A stunningly beautiful young woman with long blonde hair stands on a stone terrace of an impossibly grand pre-Flood palace, her white robes whipping in rising wind. Below the terrace, the earth is cracking open — massive fissures split the ground, and pressurised water erupts violently upward from the broken earth like geysers of biblical scale. The sky is bruised purple-black with unnatural storm clouds spiralling inward. In the far middle distance, a massive wooden ark sits in a cradle of pitch and timber, dwarfed by the scale of the cataclysm but untouched by the water. The woman's face is not terrified — it is calculating, fierce, watching the end of the world with the expression of someone already planning what comes after. The palace architecture is impossibly advanced — angular, geometric, carved from dark volcanic stone with faintly glowing violet geometric inscriptions. Dramatic chiaroscuro lighting. Hyper-detailed. Epic scale. No text or lettering.

---

### Style Notes

- **Mood:** Apocalyptic grandeur, divine judgment, defiance
- **Palette:** Deep purples, blacks, erupting white water, faint violet glow from inscriptions
- **Composition:** Woman foreground left, cracking earth centre, ark distant right
- **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9 landscape

# Map 4 — Noctis Labyrinthus, Mars
## DALL-E / Microsoft Designer Image Prompt

**Map Description:** Cross-section and floor plan of the Araqiel escape bunker within the Noctis Labyrinthus canyon system on Mars — pressurised structure, family quarters, nursery, acoustic transmitters, Cartographer's Library, sealed inner chambers of annihilation.

---

### Prompt

A dual-view technical illustration of an ancient alien bunker on Mars. The upper portion shows a cross-section side view: a pressurised structure built into the wall of a vast Martian canyon — Noctis Labyrinthus — with rust-red striated canyon walls towering above and below. The bunker is carved from dark Cydonian metamaterial, with multiple levels visible in cutaway. The lower portion shows a floor plan from above. Key areas are marked: an outer navigation-optimised corridor system (Araqiel's cartographic genius — efficient, geometric), family quarters with small rooms, a nursery with stone cribs, long-range acoustic transmitters aimed upward through the canyon wall toward Earth, a library chamber filled with cartographic scrolls and star-chart inscriptions, and at the deepest level, sealed inner chambers marked with broken ward inscriptions — the annihilation zone where Michael's judgment struck. The gate transit point is marked at the bunker entrance with a golden sigil. A dotted line traces the team's route through the facility. The Martian surface is rendered with geological accuracy — butterscotch sky, thin atmosphere, canyon geology. Architectural blueprint meets planetary science illustration. No real-world modern labels.

---

### Style Notes
- **Mood:** Alien archaeology on Mars, the poignancy of a refugee camp that became a tomb
- **Palette:** Rust-red Mars exterior, dark stone interior, amber-gold inscriptions, white broken ward marks
- **Composition:** Cross-section upper half, floor plan lower half, Mars canyon framing
- **Aspect Ratio:** 9:16 portrait (vertical cross-section emphasis)

# Map 2 — The Carpathian Domain (Armaros)
## DALL-E / Microsoft Designer Image Prompt

**Map Description:** Close-up tactical map of the Armaros domain in the Carpathian Mountains — showing the decommissioned airfield, approach routes through ward anomaly zones, subterranean domain infrastructure, stele position, guard positions, and the Voivode Dragan duel location in the hexagonal chamber.

---

### Prompt

A tactical military-style terrain map of a section of the Carpathian Mountains rendered in a blend of modern topographic precision and ancient diagrammatic overlay. The base layer shows detailed mountain topography in muted greens and greys with contour lines. Overlaid in red tactical notation: approach routes winding through mountain passes, with purple-shaded zones marking ward anomaly perimeters — areas where acoustic corruption radiates from the underground domain. A decommissioned airfield is marked on a mountain plateau. Below the surface, rendered as a translucent cross-section cutaway, the subterranean domain is visible — corridors of dark Cydonian stone descending in a spiral, chambers branching off at geometric intervals, the hexagonal central chamber marked with a red X (duel location), and the stele position marked with a golden sigil at the domain's heart. Four guard positions are marked with hostile-red diamond icons. An extraction route leads to a location marked "An Daingean." The map uses NATO tactical symbology blended with Pre-Flood geometric notation. Professional military cartography meets archaeological site plan. No real-world modern labels.

---

### Style Notes
- **Mood:** Tactical assault planning on an archaeological supernatural site
- **Palette:** Muted topographic greens/greys, red tactical overlay, purple ward zones, dark stone cutaway
- **Composition:** Surface terrain upper half, underground cutaway lower half, approach routes connecting both
- **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9 landscape

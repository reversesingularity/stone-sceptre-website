# BOOK 2 COVER CONCEPT

## "The Cauldron of God" — Cover Prompt

**Visual concept:** Cian stands at the edge of a vast circular excavation in the Antarctic ice — the Dudael site. Below him, an ancient alien structure is emerging from the ice, glowing with amber bioluminescent light. The scale is monumental. The structure was built for beings thirty feet tall. Cian is a silhouette against the amber light rising from below. Title space at top. The "cauldron" is literal — a bowl-shaped excavation in the ice, ancient evil rising from it.

**Continuity with Book 1 cover:** Same lone warrior silhouette composition. Same bronze sword. The Martian ruins of Book 1 become the Antarctic excavation of Book 2 — the threat has moved from Mars to Earth.

# PRODUCTION NOTES

## Workflow for New Chapters

When a new chapter is written, follow this process:

1. **Identify the key visual moment** — the single image that captures the chapter's emotional core
2. **Open `CHAPTER_ART_TEMPLATE.md`** — copy the BASE PROMPT
3. **Fill in the fields** using the Field Guide and Reference Palettes
4. **Generate in MJ** — aim for 4 variations (`--c 10` for slight variation)
5. **Select and upscale** the strongest result
6. **Add to this file** as a new SAMPLE section
7. **Mark as NON-CANON** until author-approved

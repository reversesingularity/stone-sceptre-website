# Epilogue — The Witnesses in Eden
## DALL-E / Microsoft Designer Image Prompt

**Chapter Highlight:** Three men in the Garden of Eden — Enoch the scribe, Elijah the fire-caller, and Dismas the redeemed thief. The two prophets stand at the Garden's shimmering boundary, preparing to leave Paradise for the 1,260-day ministry. Dismas sits beneath the Tree of Life, watching them go. He will not go with them. His commission was fulfilled on a Roman cross.

---

### Prompt

A luminous, transcendent painting of a garden that has never known autumn. The light is not sunlight — it is the original light of creation, warm and golden and falling on leaves that have never decayed, on grass that has never known frost. An enormous ancient tree dominates the centre-right — the Tree of Life, its canopy vast, its bark carrying the texture of something that predates the concept of wood, its branches heavy with fruit that glows with faint inner radiance. At the garden's far edge, two figures stand before a shimmering boundary — a distortion in the air like heat-haze, beyond which the fallen world is dimly visible as a darker, greyer reality. One figure is a robed scribe carrying a scroll, patient and ancient, five thousand years of witness in his bearing. The other is a fierce, weathered prophet in rough wilderness garments, a bronze knife sheathed at his hip, fire visible in his eyes. They face outward, toward the boundary, toward the mission. Behind them, seated beneath the Tree of Life, a third man watches them go. He is simpler — no robes of office, no weapons, no scrolls. His hands show the calluses of labour and the scars of a Roman execution. A single leaf has fallen on his shoulder. His expression carries the quiet certainty of a man who arrived in Paradise with nothing but nine words and the recognition they contained. Pre-Raphaelite luminosity meets Byzantine sacred art. No text or lettering.

### Style Notes
- **Mood:** Sacred departure, grace made visible, the fire-caller and the scribe leaving Paradise
- **Palette:** Golden creation-light, emerald unfallen green, shimmer at boundary, warm amber bark
- **Composition:** Tree of Life right, two prophets at boundary centre-left, Dismas seated beneath tree right
- **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9 landscape

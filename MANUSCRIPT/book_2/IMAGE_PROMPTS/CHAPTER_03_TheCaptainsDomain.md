# Chapter 3 — The Captain's Domain

## DALL-E / Microsoft Designer Image Prompt

**Chapter Highlight:** Cian's covert infiltration beneath the Vatican at night. He stands in St Peter's Square at 2am, looking up at the obelisk, knowing that Shemyaza's stele — the Captain of the Two Hundred's sealed domain — lies directly beneath the Basilica.

---

### Prompt

A cinematic night scene in St Peter's Square, Vatican City. A lone figure (Cian mac Morna) in a dark tactical stealth suit (splinter cell style) stands in the colonnade's shadow, looking up at the illuminated Egyptian obelisk that rises from the centre of the piazza. The Basilica of St Peter's looms behind the obelisk, its dome softly lit against a dark Roman sky. The cobblestones are wet from recent rain, reflecting the warm amber of the square's lamp-light. The figure carries something long on his back a dorsal sheath — the outline of a sword Mo Chra, faintly glowing teal through the fabric. Beneath the piazza surface, rendered as a translucent ghostly overlay visible to the viewer but not to the world, an ancient underground chamber of dark geometric stone pulses with faint violet acoustic energy — the sealed Watcher domain hidden beneath centuries of Christian architecture. The mood is covert, archaeological, the collision of ancient and modern. Painterly photorealism. Dramatic chiaroscuro. No text or lettering.

---

### Style Notes

- **Mood:** Espionage meets archaeology, sacred ground hiding something older
- **Palette:** Warm amber lamplight above, cold violet energy below, wet cobblestone reflections
- **Composition:** Cian in shadow foreground, obelisk centre, Basilica behind, ghostly domain beneath
- **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9 landscape

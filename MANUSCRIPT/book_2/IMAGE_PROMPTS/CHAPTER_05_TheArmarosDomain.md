# Chapter 5 — The Armaros Domain

## DALL-E / Microsoft Designer Image Prompt

**Chapter Highlight:** The Celestial Accord — three angelic beings negotiate above the Carpathian ridgeline at dawn. Michael (martial, absolute stillness), Raphael/The Healer (watchful, physician's attention), and the Principality of Romania (abraded, ancient, exhausted by 4,500 years guarding corrupted ground). The condition: the Healer may not intervene.

---

### Prompt

A monumental, sublime painting of three luminous beings standing above a Carpathian mountain ridgeline at pre-dawn twilight. The scene is viewed from a dramatic low angle. The central figure radiates martial authority — not carrying a sword but embodying the concept of command itself, a presence of absolute stillness and purpose, form suggesting armour and wings but transcending physical description, blazing with white-gold light. To his right, a quieter presence — watchful, attentive, the posture of a physician monitoring a distant patient, radiating warm amber-green healing light with an expression of anguished restraint. To the left, a massive, ancient presence bearing the visual weight of 4,500 years of sustained guardianship — his form showing impressions of every civilisation that sheltered beneath his jurisdiction (Dacian, Roman, Magyar, Ottoman), radiating diminished but enduring silver-blue light, abraded but not broken. Below them, the Carpathian Mountains stretch into darkness, and at the heart of the range, deep underground, a faint sickly violet pulse marks the corrupted Watcher domain. The sky transitions from deep indigo to pale gold at the horizon. Romantic sublime painting style. Caspar David Friedrich meets Byzantine iconography. No text or lettering.

---

### Style Notes

- **Mood:** Divine constitutional law, the weight of angelic jurisdiction, sublime terror
- **Palette:** White-gold (Michael), amber-green (Healer), silver-blue (Principality), violet corruption below
- **Composition:** Three figures on ridgeline upper third, mountains below, corrupted domain pulse at centre-bottom
- **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9 landscape

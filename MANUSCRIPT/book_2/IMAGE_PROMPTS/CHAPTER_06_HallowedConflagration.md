# Chapter 6 — Hallowed Conflagration

## DALL-E / Microsoft Designer Image Prompt

**Chapter Highlight:** The duel with Voivode Dragan in the hexagonal chamber — a nine-foot Nephilim champion wielding a cursed greatsword with glowing ward inscriptions against Cian and Mo Chrá. The moment Mo Chrá evolves the Chains of Abyssal Damnation — spectral chains of divine fire erupting from the blade and wrapping around the enemy.

---

### Prompt

A hyper-dramatic action painting inside a vast underground hexagonal chamber carved from dark Cydonian metamaterial stone with faintly glowing geometric inscriptions covering every surface. In the centre, two combatants clash. A nine-foot-tall pale giant of a man in ancient dark armour wields a massive cursed greatsword whose blade is inscribed with cold grey-violet glowing ward-script — he is monstrous, powerful, his face showing centuries of inhuman endurance. Facing him, a smaller but devastatingly skilled warrior in a heavy tactical exoskeleton suit holds a longsword that is erupting with supernatural energy — spectral chains of white-gold divine fire spiral outward from the blade, wrapping and coiling through the air like living things, reaching toward the giant. The chains burn with the intensity of judgment fire — Gehenna fire — casting harsh white-gold light across the entire chamber and throwing dramatic shadows. The giant's ward inscriptions pulse violet in response, trying to counter the chains. The chamber floor is cracked. The air itself seems to burn. Two other figures — a soldier with a rifle and a woman with a katana — are pressed against the chamber walls, faces lit by the supernatural conflagration. Baroque dynamism. Caravaggio meets concept art. No text or lettering.

---

### Style Notes

- **Mood:** Supernatural combat at maximum intensity, divine fire vs Watcher ward-craft
- **Palette:** White-gold Gehenna fire, grey-violet ward glow, dark Cydonian stone, dramatic shadows
- **Composition:** Duel centre, chains spiralling outward, team members at edges, hexagonal architecture framing
- **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9 landscape

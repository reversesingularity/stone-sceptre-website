## SECTION 3 — POLAR TERRAIN & ENGAGEMENT GEOMETRY

*[Agent 8 to research and populate]*

Key differences from Book 1 (Killarney / urban vampire engagement):
- **No flanks in a crevasse** — Iron Age formation instinct is a liability in narrow ice channels
- **No cover in a whiteout** — concealment depends on acoustic suppression (Shadow Hound sonic emitter + Enochian wards), not terrain
- **Ice acoustics:** Sound travels differently in ice caves — echo, reflection, and absorption profiles all change. Nephilim acoustic detection range may be significantly extended in polar cave systems.
- **Lighting:** Thermal HUD retains function; amber HUD contrast may be affected by uniform temperature environment (all surfaces near same temp)
- **Exfiltration:** No vehicles underground. Shadow Hound cannot operate in cave systems. Cian enters on foot.
- **Signature suppression:** Enochian wards mask spiritual signature; but physical heat signature (exoskeleton servo heat) may be visible in extreme cold ambient

## CRITICAL RULES FOR WRITING SESSIONS (Agent 1)

1. **Never call Mo Chrá by any other name.** It does not have a "mode" or "setting." It has a resonance state.
2. **Fuartáin** = cryo-grenades. Not "freeze grenades," not "cold bombs."
3. **Tintreach rounds** = proprietary ammunition. Never called "silver bullets" (they are copper-core with piezoelectric crystal inserts).
4. **Suppressed ≠ silent.** Suppressed firearms are still louder than most power tools. The acoustic difference matters for the Acoustic Paradigm.
5. **Reload terminology:** Cian "charges," "feeds," or "cycles" — never "reloads" in Iron Age internal monologue. Modern term surfaces in deliberate modern-context prose only.
6. **Rate of fire:** Cian never "empties" a magazine. He fires controlled bursts or deliberate single shots. 2,600 years of ammo scarcity instinct.

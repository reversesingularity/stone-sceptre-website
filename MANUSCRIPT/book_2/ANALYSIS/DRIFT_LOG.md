## RESOLVED DRIFT ISSUES

### DRIFT-001 — CH3 § VIII: Raphael Name Consequence + Wrong Jailer (HIGH × 2)
**Chapter:** CHAPTER_03_TheCaptainsDomain.md, Section VIII (The Scan), lines 537–553  
**Date detected:** 2026-03-07  
**Detected by:** Human review (author)  
**Severity:** HIGH — two inviolable canon rules broken simultaneously  
**Status:** ✅ RESOLVED (same session)

**Breach A — Name Consequence Violation (SERIES_BIBLE §4.2 / SSOT §4.2)**  
The shedim commander spoke the word *"Raphael"* aloud in Cian's presence. Per the Name Consequence rule, if Cian learns Liaigh's true identity, direct communication ends **forever**. This is a Book 5 reveal — premature disclosure collapses the entire five-book arc.

| What was written | Why it's wrong |
|---|---|
| `It spoke one word. *Raphael.*` | Cian is standing right there. He now knows Liaigh = Raphael. Series-ending breach. |

**Fix applied:** The shedim still names the angel, but speaks it in a celestial register Cian cannot parse. Mo Chrá recognises it (sword-level intelligence) but does not translate. Cian understands only that "the commander just named his angel" — identity preserved, dramatic tension retained.

**Breach B — Wrong Jailer Attribution (1 Enoch 10:11-15 / SERIES_BIBLE §5)**  
Liaigh claimed: *"I AM THE ONE WHO BOUND ITS FATHER"* — attributing Shemyaza's imprisonment to Raphael. Per 1 Enoch 10:11-15 and SERIES_BIBLE, **Michael** is the archangel who cast Shemyaza into the Abyss. Raphael bound **Azazel** in Dudael (Enoch 10:4-7). The same error appeared in the narrator's description: *"who had chained Shemyaza in the Abyss."*

| What was written | Canon truth |
|---|---|
| Liaigh: "I AM THE ONE WHO BOUND ITS FATHER" | Michael bound Shemyaza (Enoch 10:11-15) |
| Narrator: "who had chained Shemyaza in the Abyss" | Raphael bound Azazel in Dudael (Enoch 10:4-7) |

**Fix applied:** Liaigh now says: *"I BORE WITNESS AGAINST ITS FATHER BEFORE THE THRONE. I WAS PRESENT WHEN MICHAEL CAST THE CAPTAIN INTO THE ABYSS."* Narrator correctly positions Raphael as prosecutor/witness, not executioner. Michael gets proper credit.

**Root cause:** Drafting agent conflated Raphael's role (Azazel's jailer, Enoch 10:4-7) with Michael's role (Shemyaza's jailer, Enoch 10:11-15). Both are archangels acting as divine executors of judgment — easy to confuse without explicit cross-reference.

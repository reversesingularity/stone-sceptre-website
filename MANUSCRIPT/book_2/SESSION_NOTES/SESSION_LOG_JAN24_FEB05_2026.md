## NEW CANON CONFIRMED

### 1. THE CAINAN EVENT — Post-Flood Knowledge Recovery

**Source:** Book of Jubilees 8:1-5 (CANONICAL for series)

> *"And Cainan grew, and his father taught him writing, and he went to seek for himself a place where he might seize for himself a city. And he found a writing which former [generations] had carved on the rock, and he read what was thereon, and he transcribed it and sinned owing to it; for it contained the teaching of the Watchers in accordance with which they used to observe the omens of the sun and moon and stars in all the signs of heaven. And he wrote it down and said nothing regarding it; for he was afraid to speak to Noah about it lest he should be angry with him on account of it."*

| Element | Canon Definition |
|---------|------------------|
| **Identity** | Cainan ben Arphaxad — Noah's great-grandson |
| **Textual Status** | Included in Luke 3:36, LXX, Jubilees 8; OMITTED from Masoretic Genesis 11 |
| **Discovery** | Found pre-Flood rock inscriptions containing Watcher teachings |
| **Sin** | Transcribed forbidden knowledge; concealed it from Noah |
| **Consequence** | Removed from official genealogy; knowledge spread anyway |
| **Series Treatment** | Historical fact — erasure was deliberate cover-up |

**The Cainan Discrepancy Explained:**

| Source | Genealogy |
|--------|-----------|
| **Genesis 11 (Masoretic)** | Shem → Arphaxad → Shelah → Eber... |
| **Luke 3:36 (following LXX)** | Shem → Arphaxad → **CAINAN** → Shelah → Eber... |
| **Jubilees 8** | Explicitly includes Cainan with the forbidden knowledge discovery |

### 4. THE TWO LAMECHS — Genealogical Clarification

| Lamech | Father | Lineage | Significance |
|--------|--------|---------|--------------|
| **Lamech son of Methushael** | Methushael | CAIN's line (Gen 4:18) | Father of Naamah, Jabal, Jubal, Tubal-Cain |
| **Lamech son of Methuselah** | Methuselah | SETH's line (Gen 5:25-31) | Father of Noah |

**Critical Canon Point:** These are two DIFFERENT men who lived at different times.

**Timeline Resolution:**
- Cainite Lamech lived contemporary with JARED (Seth's line)
- 1 Enoch places Watcher descent during Jared's time
- Naamah's father (Cainite Lamech) was alive when Watchers descended
- This resolves the apparent timeline inconsistency

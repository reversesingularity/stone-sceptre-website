fails. Navigation degrades.

frequency filter — blocks specific signatures, passes clean ones. The team
doesn't know Michael is the reason the site is uncontested. They find it
empty. Undisturbed.

She cannot reach it. **She gave up on gate access long ago.** This is a

team anyway — not because she thinks they can activate the gate, but because
she sends House Satar after everything Cian does.

**Constitutional check:** Theologically clean (Daniel 10:13, 10:21 — Michael
as territorial principality). Acoustically grounded (counter-frequency
interdict). No Gnostic elements.

## PROPOSED GATE MECHANICS — Acoustic Resonance Depletion Model

**Constitutional amendment required:** Chris's original description used
"activation rune combination key" — visual/spatial language. Per Acoustic
Paradigm, the gate MUST activate acoustically. The runes/glyphs on the gate
are *notation* for frequencies, not a visual combination lock. Chris Guilder
reads the notation and identifies the correct acoustic sequence from the
Cydonia spectrographic data Brennan's rovers captured.

### How the Gate Works
1. **Standing wave principle:** Gate establishes a resonance between two
   points — the gate structure (transmitter) and a broadcasting stele
   (receiver/beacon).

2. **Requires a receiving signal:** Can only transit to a location actively
   broadcasting an acoustic signature. Pre-anti-singularity = all stele
   dormant = gate useless. Post-anti-singularity = all twenty broadcasting
   = destinations available. Elegantly explains 5,000 years of dormancy.

3. **Transit degrades the metamaterial:** Cydonian metamaterial frozen in
   Greenland ice for 5,000 years. Each transit causes acoustic stress
   and micro-fractures. Brennan assesses structural integrity: estimates
   **3-4 transits** before catastrophic failure.

4. **Bidirectional within a window:** Standing wave holds for a limited
   duration (~6 hours proposed). During window, transit works both ways.

5. **Each transit costs one "use":**
   - Earth → Mars = Use 1
   - Mars → Earth = Use 2 (return, or redirect to new destination)
   - Earth/Mars → Antarctica = Use 3
   - **No return trip from Antarctica.** Team is committed.

6. **Cannot be replicated:** Metamaterial is pre-Flood Watcher construction
   using frequencies post-descent Watchers can no longer produce (Oiketerion
   Principle). Brennan can analyse but cannot manufacture.

**Status: PROPOSED — awaiting author confirmation of mechanics**

### Why This Works as 5 Chapters

- **Ch5** re-engages TENSION after Ch4's QUIET. Operational mode returns.
- **Ch6** is WONDER/DREAD — the gate is awe-inspiring and terrifying.
  Includes the Brennan equipment build that Chris specified.
- **Ch7** is the Mars chapter proper — 3 functions: exploration, revelation
  (blueprints), and horror (the Araqiel clan's annihilation). Not tourism.
- **Ch8** is pure DREAD/COMBAT — the race, the wrong blueprints, the sound
  of Naamah's drill getting closer.
- **Ch9** is the climax — short, devastating, no resolution. Book ends.

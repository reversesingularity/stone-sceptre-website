## THE TWO BREACHES (DRIFT-001)

### Breach A: Name Consequence Violation

**What happened:** In Section VIII (The Scan), a shedim commander spoke the word
*"Raphael"* aloud while Cian was present and conscious. Cian heard it. Per
SERIES_BIBLE §4.2 and SSOT §4.2:

> **NAME CONSEQUENCE:** If Cian learns Liaigh's true name → Direct communication
> ends **forever**. This is a **Book 5 reveal**.

Cian learning the name "Raphael" in Chapter 3 of Book 2 would collapse the
entire five-book dramatic arc around the Name Consequence.

**The fix:** The shedim still names the angel, but speaks in a celestial register
Cian cannot interpret. Mo Chrá recognises it but does not translate. Cian
understands only that the commander *named his angel* — not which name.

### Breach B: Wrong Jailer Attribution

**What happened:** Liaigh said: *"I AM THE ONE WHO BOUND ITS FATHER"* — claiming
responsibility for imprisoning Shemyaza. The narrator repeated the error:
*"who had chained Shemyaza in the Abyss."*

**Canon truth (1 Enoch):**
- **Raphael** (Liaigh) → bound **Azazel** in Dudael (Enoch 10:4-7)
- **Michael** → cast **Shemyaza** into the Abyss (Enoch 10:11-15)

These are two different archangels executing two different divine judgments on
two different Watcher chiefs. The drafting agent conflated them.

**The fix:** Liaigh now says: *"I BORE WITNESS AGAINST ITS FATHER BEFORE THE
THRONE. I WAS PRESENT WHEN MICHAEL CAST THE CAPTAIN INTO THE ABYSS."* — correctly
positioning Raphael as prosecutor/witness, not executioner. Michael receives
proper credit.

## MANDATORY GUARDRAILS — ANGELOLOGY

**Parse these rules BEFORE writing any scene featuring Liaigh, shedim, or
Watcher/Nephilim entities.**

### Rule 1: NAME CONSEQUENCE (INVIOLABLE)

No entity — shedim, Watcher, Nephilim, Apkallu, human, or narrator — may speak,
write, signal, or otherwise convey the name "Raphael" in any form that Cian can
perceive, decode, or deduce.

**Safe patterns:**
- Shedim/entities recognise Liaigh but speak his name in a celestial register
  Cian cannot parse
- Mo Chrá may recognise the name internally but MUST NOT translate it to Cian
- Liaigh may refer to himself in relation to his actions ("I bound Azazel,"
  "I testified before the Throne") but not by his true name
- Narrator may describe Liaigh's true nature in passages Cian does not
  perceive (Mo Chrá internal, Liaigh's own thoughts) — but NEVER in Cian's POV

**Forbidden patterns:**
- Any entity saying "Raphael" where Cian can hear it
- Narrator writing "Raphael" in a Cian-POV passage without explicit framing
  that Cian does not understand the word
- Any scene where Cian logically deduces Liaigh = Raphael (this is a Book 5 event)
- Enemies taunting Cian with knowledge of his angel's identity

### Rule 2: ARCHANGEL JURISDICTIONS (INVIOLABLE)

| Archangel | Bound Which Watcher | Where | Source |
|-----------|-------------------|-------|--------|
| **Raphael** (Liaigh) | **Azazel** | Dudael (Antarctica) | 1 Enoch 10:4-7 |
| **Michael** | **Shemyaza** | The Abyss | 1 Enoch 10:11-15 |
| **Gabriel** | Nephilim (destroyed) | Pre-Flood battlefields | 1 Enoch 10:9 |

**Do NOT conflate these.** Raphael did not bind Shemyaza. Michael did not bind
Azazel. Gabriel destroyed the Nephilim offspring but did not imprison Watchers.

When writing Liaigh dialogue or narrator passages referencing the antediluvian
judgments, always cross-reference the table above.

**Raphael's relationship to Shemyaza's children:**
- Raphael **testified** against the Watchers before the Throne (witness/prosecutor)
- Raphael was **present** when Michael cast Shemyaza into the Abyss
- Raphael's **domain** is Azazel and Dudael — the Antarctic prison, not the Abyss
- The shedim fear Raphael because they remember the trial, not because he
  personally chained their father

### Rule 3: RAPHAEL'S THREE LIMITATIONS (INVIOLABLE)

| # | Limitation | Rule | Notes |
|---|-----------|------|-------|
| 1 | **THE BAN** | Cannot intervene against mortal humans | Supernatural threats only. Can advise/warn but never fight humans |
| 2 | **HEAVENLY LITURGY** | Unavailable during Tamid hours | 9-10 AM, 3-4 PM; Sabbath (limited); High Holy Days |
| 3 | **NAME CONSEQUENCE** | If Cian learns his true name → communication ends forever | Book 5 reveal. Guard this absolutely |

**Ban-compliant gray area (established in Ch3):** Liaigh CAN suppress/dampen
inanimate Watcher acoustic devices (steles, wards). These are not combatants,
not living beings — manipulation of supernatural objects is permitted.

### 2. GADREEL-EVE INCIDENT CLARIFIED

**Decision:** 1 Enoch 69:6 ("he led astray Eve") does NOT mean Gadreel was the serpent of Genesis 3.

**The Problem:**
- Genesis 3:15 (Protoevangelium) requires Satan to be the serpent
- Satan must be active to "bruise the heel" (Crucifixion) and present for head-crushing (final judgment)
- If Gadreel = serpent, he'd be in Tartarus and unable to fulfill these prophecies

**The Solution:**
Gadreel "led astray Eve" refers to a DIFFERENT incident — deceiving Eve into sanctioning weapons for Seth's line.

**The Deception:**
- Cain's line (especially Lamech) was violent and technologically advanced
- Seth's line was godly but vulnerable
- Gadreel approached Eve as a "helper," offering protection through weapons
- Eve, fearing for Seth's children, sanctioned the teaching
- Result: Arms race → corruption of BOTH lines → "earth filled with violence" → Flood

**Eve's Two Falls:**

| First Fall (Satan) | Second Fall (Gadreel) |
|-------------------|----------------------|
| Promised WISDOM | Promised PROTECTION |
| Desire for knowledge | Fear for children |
| Took forbidden fruit | Sanctioned forbidden knowledge |
| Spiritual death entered | Physical death industrialized |

**Eve is NOT:**
- Sexually involved with any Watcher
- Subject to Siren transformation
- Responsible for the Watcher rebellion

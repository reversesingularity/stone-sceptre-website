## DISMAS ARC (Books 3–5)

Dismas is the theological keystone of the final three books:

- **Book 3 Ch 9:** Speaks — addresses Cian's worthiness crisis directly; "You think you need to earn it. I'm the man who didn't have time."
- **Book 3 Epilogue Mvt II:** Begins a vigil of prayer at the Eden gate. Stays.
- **Book 4 (background):** The vigil continues through all 1,260 days. Never shown but always present.
- **Book 5 Ch 6:** The vigil is visible — three and a half days left; Dismas still at prayer.
- **Book 5 Ch 7:** Defends Eden against the Dragon's assault with pure testimony (Rev 12:17). No weapon. No power. Only the grace he received for free.
- **Book 5 Epilogue:** Nods at Cian as the gate opens. Everything said in Ch 9 is now fully lived.
